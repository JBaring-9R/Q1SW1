My Favorite Game: Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is a free-to-play action role-playing game that takes place in the fantasy world of Teyvat, which is home to seven nations, ruled by different gods and goddesses. This game is developed and published by Hoyoverse back in September 28, 2020.

Why I like Genshin Impact

  • Beautiful scenery
  • Nice background music
  • Very cool lore
